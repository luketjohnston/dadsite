About

Scott Johnston received his BFA from Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, California, and has been a professional artist for over 30 years. His art hangs in numerous public and private collections. He is a member of the Portrait Society of America and Oil Painters of America.
“It is art that makes life, makes interest, makes importance, and I know no substitute for the force and beauty of its process.” —Henry James
